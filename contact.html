Frequently Asked Questions

1

How to donate?

Find the donate page link here and fill the necessary details.

2

What type of medicine?

Any surplus medicine which is not expired.

3

Who can donate?

Any individual or any organization can donate.

4

For whom are we donating?

We will donate the medicines to the NGOs that have registerd in our portal.

5

How the donation process will be carried on?

After the donator enters all the necessary details, our volunteer will collect the medicine manually from the given address, the same medicine will be checked for expiration date by our authorized Pharmacist, once it satisfies all the condition, it will be delivered to the NGOs.

6

What if anyone donates expired medicine?

As we all know that expired medicines are harmful to our health, we will not return it to the donator, instead we throw/destory and we will make sure nobody consumes those medicine.
